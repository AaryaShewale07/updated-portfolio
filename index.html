About Me

I’m Aarya, a Software Developer & UI/UX Designer who enjoys turning rough ideas into structured, usable, and visually clean interfaces.

Who I Am

I’m currently pursuing B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering with a specialization in AI & ML at Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj University, Panvel. I work at the intersection of design and development: I design flows in Figma and actually implement them using modern web tech.

How I Work

I focus on user-centered design, clean information hierarchy, and responsive layouts. My process usually goes:

  • Understand the problem & user needs
  • Wireframe flows and key screens
  • Design high-fidelity UI in Figma
  • Implement using React / MERN where needed

What I’m Exploring

AI-enabled interfaces, gesture-based interaction systems, and IoT dashboards for real-time sensor data. I like projects where design and engineering both actually matter.
